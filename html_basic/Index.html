Chelsea Football Club

Chealse Football Club is one of the most successful football clubs in the world and today, we are going to be tasking a deep dive into everything about their history, inckluding creation and successes.

Creation

Chelsea Football Club were founded on 10 March 1905 at The Rising Sun pub, (now The Butcher's Hook) opposite today's main entrance to the ground on the Fulham Road.

Names Suggested Before The Adoption Of Chealsea FC

There was already a team named Fulham FC, so a few names were suggested before the adoption of Chelsea FC as the club's name. These included:

  • London FC
  • Kensington FC and
  • Stamford Bridge FC

Major Trophies Won

Domestically, Chelsea have won six top-flight titles, eight FA Cups and five League Cups. In international competitions, they have won two UEFA Champions League titles, two UEFA Europa Leagues, two UEFA Cup Winners' Cups, two UEFA Super Cups, one UEFA Conference League and one FIFA Club World Cup.
